Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the DSR App ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Data Collection & Storage

The DSR App is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

All data you enter into the app—including project details, personnel information, daily site reports, manpower statistics, and generated PDF/CSV files—is stored locally entirely on your device using local storage.

2. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions to function properly:

  • Storage/Files: To save and export generated Daily Site Reports (PDF and CSV files) directly to your device.
  • Camera/Gallery (Optional): To allow you to attach site photos to your daily reports. Photos are embedded locally into your generated PDFs and are never uploaded to any cloud server by us.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party open-source libraries (such as Chart.js for analytics and jsPDF for PDF generation) which run entirely locally on your device. We do not use any third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising software that collects user data.

4. Data Security

Because your data never leaves your device, the security of your data is governed by the security of your own device. We recommend keeping a screen lock on your device to protect your local application data.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Since the app operates locally, any changes to data handling would only occur if you update the app via the Google Play Store.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the official app listing page.
